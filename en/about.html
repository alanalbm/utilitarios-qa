About

A portal for solving tasks and sharing practical knowledge.

Utilitários QA brings together tools for documents, images, data, development, productivity and software quality. The goal is to reduce repetitive work without requiring an account.

Mission

Provide clear, fast and useful utilities for people who build, test, study, document and organize digital work.

How it works

The site is static and hosted on GitHub Pages. Operations marked as local run in your browser.

What makes it different

  • 127 tools organized around tasks.
  • QA content linked to examples, templates and interactive tools.
  • Portuguese, English and Spanish versions with separate URLs.
  • Transparent limitations, privacy and methodology.
